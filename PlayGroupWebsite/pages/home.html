East Craigs Playgroup

Local Mother And Toddler Playgroup

East Craigs Playgroup is a local mother and toddler playgroup for children aged 2½ to 5 years.

Sessions run on Monday to Friday mornings, 9.15 am to 11.15 am at East Craigs Church hall.

The Playgroup provides a safe and fun environment for children to experience many types of play, develop skills and make friends.
